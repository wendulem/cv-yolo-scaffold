Challenges we faced


Our primary issue was producing clean and pure data that would ensure the model ran efficiently. Using Labelbox, we were able to label our data by drawing squares identifying the toxicity of the plant, around parts of it. We ran into issues when some of our images were not labelled clearly, were blurry, or overall unclean. This led to issues in training the model, and eventually us precisely relabelling all of our data.
